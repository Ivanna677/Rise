Know Your Enemy

Cyborgs are built to endure extreme damage and remain operational under most conditions. Their armor is made from an alien alloy that resists bullets, plasma, and even EMPs. But no machine is perfect. They all have weaknesses.

The primary vulnerability is the synthetic heart unit located in the chest cavity. While most of the torso is armored, this unit remains partially exposed due to its high energy output and cooling requirements. A direct strike to this area can cause the entire system to overheat and fail.

Another weak point is the partially unprotected face. Unlike the rest of their body, the area around the mouth and jaw lacks full shielding. This is where their neuro-signal receiver is embedded. A well-placed electric surge, acid, or blunt force trauma can disable their ability to process commands.
